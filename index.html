  • How many attempts are permitted for the CA Exam?

    The number of times a candidate may take the CA Foundation and Intermediate tests is restricted to a maximum of six, but the number of times a candidate may take the CA Final exams is not capped. Yet, in order to prevent any delays in the completion of the program, it is strongly suggested to pass the examinations on the first or second try.

  • IS CA Syllabus Change in 2023?

    ICAI has decreased the number of CA Final papers from 8 to 6 as part of the revised CA program for 2023. At this point, there are two groups, each of which will have three papers. The following are some of the significant changes made to the CA Final Subjects: The topics that were covered in papers 4 and 5 are going to be relocated to the self-paced modules instead. On the other hand, it is strongly advised to check the ICAI website as well as any official notifications often for any modifications or revisions.

  • Which Subject is tough in CA?

    The degree of difficulty of the courses covered in the CA program might vary greatly from student to student. On the other hand, many students feel that Advanced Accounting and Financial Reporting are substantially more difficult than the other disciplines since they need a deeper grasp of accounting rules, ideas, and principles.

  • Is there any negative marking for wrong answers to MCQs in CS Examinations?

    The 1st Stage i.e. CSEET examination is MCQ-based with no negative marking for any incorrect answer.

    Further, some papers of CS Executive Examinations are MCQ-based with a negative marking for any wrong answer attempted by the candidates. For every incorrect answer, 0.25 mark will be deducted.

  • How many attempts are permitted for the CS Exam?

    Students attempting for the CSEET are allowed 6 attempts, while students giving examinations for CS executives are allowed 10 attempts. A student trying for the CS Professional Examination is allowed 10 attempts.

  • How to answer the case study in company law?

    You have to write answer in 3 paragraphs.

    1. Facts of the case
    2. Provisions of the case
    3. Conclusion

    It helps to improve clarity and presentation of answer

  • Can a student appearing for graduation final year exam register directly for the Executive Programme?

    No, provisional admissions are not allowed for the Executive Programme. Only upon completion of graduation with a minimum of 50%, a student can enroll directly for Executive Programme.
